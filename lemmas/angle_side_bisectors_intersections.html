Proposition:
  • Intersection \(N_a\) of angle bisector \(t_a\) and side bisector \(b_a\) lies on the circumcircle \(o\).
  • Intersection \(N_b\) of angle bisector \(t_b\) and side bisector \(b_b\) lies on the circumcircle \(o\).
  • Intersection \(N_c\) of angle bisector \(t_c\) and side bisector \(b_c\) lies on the circumcircle \(o\).
