Altitude feet

Proposition:
  • Altitude feet \(H_b\) and \(H_c\) belong to the circle \(c_{Ma}\) over segment \(BC\) i.e., the circle centered at midpoint \(M_a\) of the segment \(BC\), that contains \(B\) and \(C\).
  • Altitude feet \(H_a\) and \(H_c\) belong to the circle \(c_{Mb}\) over segment \(AC\) i.e., the circle centered at midpoint \(M_b\) of the segment \(AC\), that contains \(A\) and \(C\).
  • Altitude feet \(H_a\) and \(H_b\) belong to the circle \(c_{Mc}\) over segment \(AB\) i.e., the circle centered at midpoint \(M_c\) of the segment \(AB\), that contains \(A\) and \(B\).
